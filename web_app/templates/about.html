About Me

Hi there! My name is Darshil Shah. I am a senior in the MSB at Georgetown University, studying OPIM and Finance. I am currently taking a class called Mgmt. App Development in Python (OPIM 244). As a part of this class, I have taken on a project to develop a new app.

This app is inspired by one of my elementry school French teachers. I started learning the French language early in my school life and I love the language and my love for the language can be attributed to her only!

At the time of developing this app, I spoke to her about some of the challenges that she is facing during the COVID crisis. To help her and her students, I decided to create an app that helps the students practice their French while they are at home.

If you have any feedback or suggestions about this app, I'd love to hear from you! Please don't hesitate to reach out to me.
