Game instructions

Here's what you need to know:

  1. Click on the "Home" button in the navigation bar above.
  2. Once you arrive at the home page, click on "New Quiz" to being setting up a quiz
  3. Once you arrive at the game page, select your level, category and quiz length
  4. Once you have done so, click on "Start Quiz" to begin the quiz
  5. Enter your responses and click the "Submit" button at the end of the quiz

If you're still confused, go ahead, start a new quiz and learn as you move along!

You can also find out more about the app's features on the home page
